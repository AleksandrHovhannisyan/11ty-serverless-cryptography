--- id: caesar title: Caesar Cipher description: A weak substitution cipher that shifts a plaintext alphabet by a certain number of places to the right to produce a cipher alphabet. This alphabet is then used to encipher and decipher messages. Of particular note is a Caesar cipher of shift 13, known as rot13, because enciphering and deciphering are symmetric operations. infoUrl: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Caesar_cipher permalink: /caesar/ ---
